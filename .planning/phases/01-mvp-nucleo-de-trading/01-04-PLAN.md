Supabase Auth flows (RESEARCH Integration Touchpoint 1):
- register: supabase.auth.signUp({email,password}) → verification email
- login: supabase.auth.signInWithPassword({email,password}) → session.access_token
- oauth: supabase.auth.signInWithOAuth({provider:'google'|'github'}) → redirect → /auth/callback
- reset: supabase.auth.resetPasswordForEmail(email)
- session refresh: supabase.auth.onAuthStateChange(listener)
- logout all (AUT-04): supabase.auth.signOut({scope:'global'})
